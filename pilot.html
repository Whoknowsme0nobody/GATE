Become a GATE Pilot Tester

Help us shape the future of standardized testing by participating in our pilot program

Why Become a Pilot Tester?

Shape the Future of Testing

Your feedback will directly influence the development of a more equitable and accessible standardized test that could benefit millions of students worldwide.

Early Access

Be among the first to experience the GATE exam before it's publicly available.

Contribute to Change

Play a direct role in creating a more accessible alternative to expensive standardized tests.

Recognition

Receive acknowledgment for your contribution to educational equity in our official documentation.

What to Expect:

  • Participate in a 2-hour 55-minute modular exam (Reading & Writing + Mathematics)
  • Experience built-in breaks between modules for a more comfortable testing environment
  • Provide feedback on question quality, difficulty, and overall experience
  • Help us refine timing, scoring, and test structure
  • Contribute to making standardized testing more accessible for students worldwide

Important Notes:

The English Communication section has been postponed to Phase 2 development. Pilot testing will focus on the Reading & Writing and Mathematics sections.

All pilot testers will receive a certificate of participation and early access to future GATE materials.
