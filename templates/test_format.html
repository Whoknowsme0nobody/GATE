Test Format

Below is a breakdown of the GATE test sections, along with descriptions and maximum points for each section.

Reading & Writing

Assesses comprehension and writing skills.

Max Points: 25

Sample Question: What is the main theme of the passage?

Math

Tests mathematical reasoning and problem-solving skills.

Max Points: 25

Sample Question: Solve for x: 2x + 3 = 7

English Communication

Evaluates speaking and listening skills.

Max Points: 40

Sample Question: Summarize the main points of the conversation.

Global Mind

Assesses critical thinking and global awareness.

Max Points: 10

Sample Question: Discuss the impact of technology on society.
