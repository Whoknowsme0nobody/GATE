About GATE

GATE is a nonprofit initiative dedicated to creating an affordable, global standardized test that combines academic skills and English proficiency. Our mission is to provide accessible and fair testing opportunities for students around the world.

The Problem

Current tests like SAT/ACT and TOEFL are expensive and often inaccessible to many students. They also do not adequately assess the global skills needed in today's interconnected world.

How GATE Addresses the Issue

GATE aims to provide a more affordable and fair alternative. Our test covers a wide range of skills, ensuring that every student has the opportunity to showcase their abilities.
