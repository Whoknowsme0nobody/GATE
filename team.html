Join the GATE Team

Be part of the movement to make standardized testing more equitable and accessible

Why Join Us?

At GATE, we believe that education should be accessible to everyone, regardless of their financial background or geographic location. Our team is made up of passionate students, educators, and advocates who are committed to breaking down barriers in standardized testing.

By the people. For the future.

GATE is a student-led initiative that's creating real change in education. When you join our team, you're not just volunteering - you're becoming part of a movement that's reshaping the future of standardized testing.

Our Core Values

Equity

We're committed to creating opportunities for all students, regardless of their background.

Innovation

We're reimagining what standardized testing can be with fresh ideas and modern approaches.

Collaboration

We believe in the power of teamwork and diverse perspectives to solve complex problems.

Integrity

We maintain the highest standards of honesty and transparency in everything we do.

Team Roles

Content Development

Help create and review test questions, passages, and materials for our Reading & Writing and Mathematics sections.

Technical Support

Assist with website development, platform testing, and technical infrastructure for our online exam system.

Marketing & Outreach

Spread the word about GATE through social media, partnerships, and community engagement.

Research & Analysis

Conduct research on educational equity, analyze pilot test data, and contribute to our evidence-based approach.

What We Offer

  • Meaningful volunteer experience that contributes to educational equity
  • Flexible time commitments to fit your schedule
  • Opportunities to develop professional skills in education, technology, and nonprofit work
  • Networking with like-minded individuals passionate about social change
  • Recognition for your contributions in our official documentation
  • Early access to GATE materials and developments

Ready to Make a Difference?

Join our team of passionate volunteers working to create a more equitable future for standardized testing. Whether you're a student, educator, developer, or advocate, we have a place for you.
